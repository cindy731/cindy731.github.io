CORONAVIRUS

KEY FACTS

Click on each puzzle piece to see the whole picture

WHAT IS IT?

Coronavirus (also known as COVID-19) is a respiratory disease caused by a virus.

SYMPTOMS

May appear 2 - 14 days after exposure

  • Coughing
  • Fever
  • Shortness of breath

Severe complications

  • Pneumonia and organ failure

RISK FACTORS

People at higher risk for serious illness

  • Age 65+
  • Living in nursing home/long-term care facility
  • Underlying health conditions

GLOBAL IMPACT

Worldwide, there has been:

  • 3,207,285 cases
  • 222,369 deaths
  • 996,959 recovered

Only 18 countries have not reported any cases

HOW DOES IT SPREAD?

Through respiratory droplets spread between people in close contact when an infected person coughs or sneezes.

PROTECT YOURSELF AND OTHERS

  • Prevention is key!
  • Stay at home
  • Practice social distancing (stay at least 6 feet from others and avoid crowds)
  • Wear a cloth mask when around others (do not use masks meant for health workers!)
  • Wash your hands often
  • Avoid touching your face
  • Clean and disinfect surfaces regularly
